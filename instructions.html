STORY

There are two mafia groups - The Obsidian Brotherhood and The Midnight Ravens. These two groups are fighting to decide who will rule the underworld. Both groups send their best representative, who then engage in a thrilling duel to till one of the fighter reaches death. The mafia group of the survivor gains a point. The representative from the Obsidian Brotherhood is Player 1 in the game and the representative from the Midnight Ravens is Player 2 in the game.
